Cultural Showcase

The Cultural showcase is for students to exhibit their culturally diverse talents and gifts to the other conference participants. If you would like to perform, please complete this form to tell us about your performance by Wednesday, February 13 2019. We strongly encourage live performances such as dances, singing acts, demonstrations and short theatre performances rather than projected presentations. Your performance MUST adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Act CANNOT exceed five minutes! (in these five minutes you must set time aside for set up and clean up)
  • Media to be used in the performance must be submitted via email prior to the performance on the completed form.
  • Schools may only perform ONE time total during the show.
  • NO foul/insensitive language, offensive or inappropriate gestures.
  • NO pyrotechnics, open flames or any other dangerous additions and props.
  • Acts are responsible for their own recorded music, props, instruments, etc., if needed.
  • Recorded video files MUST be sent in advance, via email, to info@unis-un.org in an acceptable format (i.e. AVI or MOV)
  • Recorded music MUST be sent in advance, via email, to info@unis-un.org in an acceptable format (i.e. MP3 or WAV)
